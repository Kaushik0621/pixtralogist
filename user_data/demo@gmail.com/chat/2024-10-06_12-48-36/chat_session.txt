### **Mediterranean Chickpea Salad**

#### **Ingredients:**

- **Salad:**
  - 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  - 1 small cucumber, diced
  - 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  - 1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
  - 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  - 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  - 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped

- **Dressing:**
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 clove garlic, minced
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - Salt and pepper to taste

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Dressing:**
   - In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, honey or maple syrup, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

2. **Prepare the Salad:**
   - In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, diced cucumber, diced red bell pepper, chopped red onion, Kalamata olives, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped parsley, and chopped mint.

3. **Assemble the Salad:**
   - Pour the dressing over the salad.
   - Gently toss to combine, ensuring all ingredients are well coated with the dressing.

#### **Nutrition Facts (Approximate):**
- Calories: ~350
- Protein: 13g
- Carbohydrates: 45g
- Fat: 14g
- Fiber: 12g

### **Why This Recipe is Suitable:**
1. **Low Calorie:** This salad is packed with fiber and nutrients but keeps the calories in check.
2. **Lactose-Free:** It avoids dairy products, making it suitable for lactose intolerance.
3. **High in Fiber:** Chickpeas and vegetables provide plenty of fiber, which aids in satiety and digestion.
4. **Balanced Macronutrients:** The combination of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats helps maintain stable blood sugar levels and keeps you full for longer.

### **Additional Tips:**

- For added crunch, include some roasted chickpeas or other low-calorie roasted vegetables.
