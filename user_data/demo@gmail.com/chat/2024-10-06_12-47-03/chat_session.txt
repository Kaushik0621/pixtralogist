### **Grilled Chicken Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette**

#### **Ingredients:**
- **Salad:**
  - 3 oz (85g) grilled chicken breast
  - 2 cups mixed greens (e.g., spinach, kale, arugula)
  - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  - 1/4 cup bell pepper, sliced
  - 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  - 2 tbsp sliced red onion

- **Lemon Vinaigrette:**
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - Salt and pepper to taste

#### **Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Lemon Vinaigrette:**
   - In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey or maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

2. **Prepare the Salad:**
   - In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, shredded carrots, and red onion.

3. **Assemble the Salad:**
   - Top the mixed greens with the grilled chicken breast.
   - Drizzle the lemon vinaigrette over the salad.
   - Toss gently to combine.

#### **Nutrition Facts (Approximate):**
- Calories: ~250
- Protein: 25g
- Carbohydrates: 15g
- Fat: 12g
- Fiber: 3g
